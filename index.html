Unfinished Business

  • Defending as a unit. Our back-line is still un-coordinated and not moving through positions together. But to be fair however, this can be excused since we have gone through about 10 different iterations of a back 4 because of our unprecedented injury crisis.

  • Conceding less than 20 shots in the game. We have conceded the most shots of any other team in the Premier League this season and clearly have not learnt our lesson yet. The reason for this also boils down to the fact that our midfielders (again today) did not track runners near the box and stay with their man.
